About CSI

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is the largest professional association of computer and information technology professionals in India. Established in 1965, CSI aims to promote the advancement of computer science and information technology through knowledge sharing, research and professional development.CSI provides a platform for students, academicians, researchers, and industry professionals to enhance their technical skills and stay updated with emerging technologies. The organization conducts seminars, workshops, conferences and technical events that encourage innovation, learning, and collaboration.Through its nationwide network of chapters and student branches, CSI works toward strengthening the IT community and promoting the effective use of technology for the benefit of society.

Mission

  • Promote the advancement of computer science and information technology in India.
  • Encourage research, innovation, and knowledge sharing among IT professionals and students.
  • Support continuous learning and professional development through technical activities.
  • Provide a platform for collaboration between academia and industry.
  • Develop technical, professional, and leadership skills among students and members.
  • Promote the effective and ethical use of technology for the benefit of society

Vision

  • To be a leading professional body advancing the field of information technology in India
  • To promote excellence in education, research, and innovation in computing
  • To create a strong and skilled IT community through knowledge sharing and collaboration
  • To encourage the responsible and ethical use of technology for societal development
  • To empower students and professionals to contribute to technological growth and digital transformation

CSI Student Chapter in Our College

The Computer Society of India (CSI) Student Chapter in our college provides a platform for students to enhance their technical knowledge and practical skills in computer science and information technology. The chapter organizes workshops, seminars, coding competitions, and technical events that encourage innovation, teamwork and continuous learning. It helps students connect academic learning with industry requirements and supports their overall professional development.
